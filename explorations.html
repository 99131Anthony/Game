Board Game Exploration

Me and my group worked on a board game together that involves dices and blockers. The game can be played with 2 to 4 players. The goal is to get your piece to the center space. You use one dice to determine how much spaces you move.

I made a sprite that moved across the screen to the right and would go back to the left side of the screen, all of this in a loop.
