Board Game Exploration

Me and my group worked on a board game together that involves dices and blockers. The game can be played with 2 to 4 players. The goal is to get your piece to the center space. You use one dice to determine how much spaces you move. The pieces start on the outside circle three spaces apart from each other. At the start of the game you can two blockers which can be placed anywhere, in order to use it you need to roll a one. If you decide to place a blcok then you can't move for that turn. When you are clsoe to the center space you need an exact roll to win.

I made a sprite that moved across the screen to the right and would go back to the left side of the screen, all of this in a loop.
